Blue Tit

Description

A very small blue, green and yellow bird commonly found in gardens, parks and woodlands. Juveniles have yellow on their heads, Male and female are similar.

  • All year
  • Gardens & forests
  • Very small (12cm)

Spotting Tip

Distinguishable from the Great Tit by its blue head, the Blue Tit is smaller and more similar in size to the Coal Tit.
