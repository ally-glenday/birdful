Great Tit

Description

A large brightly-coloured tit, the Great Tit is a very common bird found in gardens, parks and woodlands all over the UK. Male and female are similar, though males have a thicker black band down their yellow chest.

  • All year
  • Gardens & forests
  • Very small (14cm)

Spotting Tip

Much larger than the Blue Tit and the Coal Tit, Great Tits can be identified by their thick black band down their yellow chest.
