Long-Tailed Tit

Description

These tiny-bodied, tiny-billed black, grey and pink birds have long, slim tails and small, rounded heads. Long-Tailed Tits are very cute, fluffy birds and are very social and usually found in flocks.

  • All year
  • Gardens & forests
  • Very small (11cm)

Spotting Tip

Long-Tailed Tits are quite unique in their looks, their small frame and long tails are quite distinctive. They are often seen in groups
