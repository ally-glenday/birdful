Robin

Description

One of the most recognisable garden birds, Robins are melodious song birds that are found across gardens, parks and woodloods in the UK.

  • All year
  • Gardens & forests
  • Very small (14cm)

Spotting Tip

Tree Sparrows are distinguishable by their brown cap on their head and shorter tail. Slightly smaller than the House Sparrow, Tree Sparrows have black on their chins like male House Sparrows.
