Tree Sparrow

Description

Much less common than the House Sparrow, Tree Sparrows are often found in woodlands, but are also more recently found in urban areas like gardens and parks.

  • All year
  • Gardens & forests
  • Very small (14cm)

Spotting Tip

Tree Sparrows are distinguishable by their brown cap on their head and shorter tail. Slightly smaller than the House Sparrow, Tree Sparrows have black on their chins like male House Sparrows.
